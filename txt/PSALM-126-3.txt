3. Let <i>those who sow</i> their seed <i>in</i> tears,
beset by <i>anxious cares and</i> fears,
at <i>harvest time no</i> longer weep,
re<i>joicing in the</i> crops they reap.
The <i>sower going forth in</i> sorrow
to <i>carry seed to field and</i> furrow
will <i>with his sheaves come</i> home again,
ex<i>ulting in the</i> golden grain.
