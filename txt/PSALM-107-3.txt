3. Some, wandering <i>in waste </i>places,
found <i>nowhere </i>they could stay.
God heard them <i>and was </i>gracious;
he <i>led them </i>on their way.
With <i>thirst and </i>hunger weak,
they <i>cried to God to </i>save them
from <i>deserts </i>dry and bleak.
A <i>place of rest he </i>gave them.
