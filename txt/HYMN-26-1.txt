1. You, holy Lamb of God, we bless.
You, through your cross, redemption sent us,
and to the Father you present us
as priests and kings in holiness.
O Saviour, you have ransomed us.
Hence we will honour and adore you,
and cast in gratitude before you
the crowns by grace bestowed on us.
Amen, Amen. Hallelujah!
