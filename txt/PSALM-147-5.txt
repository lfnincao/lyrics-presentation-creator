5. When he the <i>wintry cold in</i>creases,
he <i>spreads the </i>snow like <i>woolly </i>fleeces;
like <i>ashes </i>he the <i>hoarfrost </i>scatters,
and <i>hailstones </i>on the <i>earth he </i>clatters.
Be<i>fore his </i>cold the <i>water </i>freezes
till <i>he the icy bonds re</i>leases.
