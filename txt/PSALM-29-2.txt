2. Cedars <i>shatter, </i>forests fall,
mountains <i>shudder at his </i>call.
Like <i>a </i>calf <i>leaps Leba</i>non,
like <i>a </i>wild-<i>ox Siri</i>on.
God's voice, <i>flames from heaven </i>flashing,
with the <i>roar of thunder </i>crashing,
shakes the <i>wilderness, and </i>broken
lie <i>its </i>oaks <i>when he has </i>spoken.
