6. Life <i>is like </i>grass, so <i>quick to fade and </i>perish,
or <i>like a </i>flower that <i>will but briefly </i>flourish,
<i>that </i>sears and withers <i>in the blowing </i>wind;
soon <i>it is </i>gone, not <i>leaving any </i>traces.
But <i>God is </i>ever <i>merciful and </i>gracious:
<i>his </i>love stands firm, a<i>biding without </i>end.
