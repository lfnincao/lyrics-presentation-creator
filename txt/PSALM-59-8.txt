8. You <i>will at daybreak hear me </i>singing,
to <i>you, O God, my praises </i>bringing,
for <i>I will glory </i>in your might
and <i>in your steadfast </i>love delight.
You <i>are my </i>strength, my <i>rock and </i>tower,
my <i>refuge in the darkest </i>hour.
Your <i>praise I sing and </i>shout abroad,
O <i>mighty Fortress</i>, loving God!
