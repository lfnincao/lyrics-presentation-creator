6. Mighty <i>waters writhed and </i>trembled,
for they <i>saw you and were </i>humbled;
fear con<i>vulsed the hidden </i>deep,
made the <i>roaring billows </i>leap.
Clouds poured <i>rain, with thunder </i>crashing,
and your <i>arrows kept on </i>flashing.
With the <i>lightning bolts you </i>hurled,
you lit <i>up and shook the </i>world.
