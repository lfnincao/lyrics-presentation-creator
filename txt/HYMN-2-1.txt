1. In God, our heavenly Father, I believe -
almighty Ruler, all-victorious,
Creator of both heaven and earth,
whose holy name is great and glorious.
And I believe in Christ our Lord -
Christ Jesus, the incarnate Word,
God's only Son, our faithful Saviour,
sent to redeem a world forlorn;
who was conceived by God the Spirit
and of the virgin Mary born.
